If you do live in a floodplain, consider buying flood insurance to help with losses if, and when, a flood occurs. Construct barriers (levees, beams, floodwalls) to stop floodwater from entering your home. Sandbags can provide a temporary levee in an emergency. Seal walls in basements with waterproofing compounds to avoid seepage. Pay attention to weather forecasts. When heavy rain or storms are forecasted, listen to the radio or television for information on flooding risk.
